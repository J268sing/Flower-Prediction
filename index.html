About

This Web app takes in a flower image and uses a deep learning model to predict name of the species of flower that is the nearest look-alike (neighbour) of flower present in the image, out of 102 flower species on which the the model has been trained on.

The model requires that the user must input an image that has flower in it. Otherwise, it will assume the presence of flower and will give the nearest look-alike.
